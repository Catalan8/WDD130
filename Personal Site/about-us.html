Our Story

I created Scrubby Dubby Sugar Scrubs for those like me who suffer from embarrassing, flaky skin. After moving from sunny California to dry and frigid Idaho I experienced parched, cracked hands. Luckily, I found the solution in sugar scrubs.

Now I share the gift of radiant skin with others. Because when you look good, smell good, and feel good you can accomplish great things!
