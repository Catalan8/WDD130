Purpose

My purpose of this site is to educate a prospect on why they should by a sugar scrub and specifically a sugar scrub from Scrubby Dubby Scrubs.

Audience

Women from 21-30 years old. They are frequent Etsy and farmers market shoppers. They have a bachlors degree and are yound professionals. They put a lot of effort and mony into their skincare routine. They seek out quality brands. They enjoy shopping, social media, the latest tv shows, and makeup tutorials.
